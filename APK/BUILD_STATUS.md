### What's in v0.5.0
- Main menu with dark medieval theme
- Campaign map with 10 connected nodes (battles, camps, outposts, boss)
- Real-time auto-battle system with Canvas-rendered battlefield
- 5 player intervention commands (Focus, Push, Hold, Rally, Retreat)
- 19 unit types across 5 categories (Frontline, Ranged, Skirmish, Cavalry, Support)
- Warband management with recruitment and squad expansion
- Battle results with rewards (supplies + renown)
- Recovery camps for healing
- Faction outposts for recruiting
- Regional boss encounter as win condition
- Morale system affecting combat effectiveness
- How to Play guide
